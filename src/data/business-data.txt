# Community Information

This is a Learning Management System (LMS) community platform designed to facilitate educational collaboration and knowledge sharing among members.

## Features
- Interactive chat bot for community assistance
- User-friendly interface for learning
- Community engagement and support
- Resource sharing and collaboration tools
