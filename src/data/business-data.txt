Anyone Can Dance (ACD) is a live, online dance learning platform created to make dance accessible to everyone. As a part of SoulSkool—a creative space that blends wellness and performing arts—ACD brings a unique mix of rhythm, emotion, and transformation into every session. We offer real-time sessions that go far beyond choreography, focusing on emotional expression, confidence building, and physical well-being.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

Q: Do I need prior dance experience?
A: No! Anyone Can Dance is designed for complete beginners. Our motto is "Anyone Can Dance" - we welcome everyone regardless of experience level.

Q: What do I need to participate?
A: Just your energy and a little space to move. You can join from anywhere in the world with an internet connection.

Q: How long are the sessions?
A: Each session is 30 minutes, making it easy to fit into your daily routine.

Q: When do classes start?
A: Our 5-Day Workshop starts every Monday, with both morning and evening batch options.

Q: Are classes recorded?
A: No, all our sessions are live for personalized interaction and real-time feedback. This ensures you get the most engaging and effective learning experience.

Q: What age groups can join?
A: We welcome participants from ages 4 to 70+. Dance truly is for everybody!

Q: What makes ACD different from other dance classes?
A: We combine dance with therapy, wellness, and community building. It's not just about learning steps - it's about transformation, confidence building, and feeling alive through movement.

Q: Can I get certified?
A: Yes, we offer progressive learning paths with certification opportunities as you advance in your dance journey.

Q: Is this suitable for fitness?
A: Absolutely! Our dance sessions provide excellent physical exercise while being fun and engaging.

Q: How do I join the 5-Day Workshop?
A: Visit our website at https://anyonecandance.in or https://learn.anyonecandance.in/acd to register for the next Monday's workshop.

Anyone Can Dance is India's leading online dance training platform, helping over 1 Lakh people go from hesitant beginners to confident dancers. We believe dance is for everyone-no matter your age or experience.

I'm Tiru Sameer, a Dance Coach and Choreo Specialist with 24 years of experience. From self-taught beginnings to creating my own style at 16, my journey has been about one thing-using dance as a tool for transformation.
